# 《锈湖：炼金术士的天体仪》美术风格核心提示词

## 1. 核心风格基石 (Core Style Foundation)

这是所有美术资产生成的基础，旨在确立统一的“锈湖”式视觉语言。所有提示词都应以此为根基进行扩展。

**基础风格提示词 (Base Style Prompt):**
`In the distinct art style of the Rusty Lake series, a detailed hand-drawn illustration with a printmaking texture. The scene is characterized by a muted and desaturated color palette, dramatic chiaroscuro lighting, and a surreal, mysterious atmosphere. Etching-style cross-hatching creates deep shadows, set against a textured, slightly worn paper background.`

**中文解析:**
`以锈湖系列独特的艺术风格，一幅细节丰富的手绘插画，带有版画质感。场景特点是柔和且低饱和度的色调，戏剧性的明暗对比光照，以及超现实、神秘的氛围。蚀刻风格的交叉排线绘制出深邃的阴影，背景是带有纹理、略显陈旧的纸张。`

---

## 2. 模块化提示词库 (Modular Prompt Library)

根据资产类型，将以下模块与核心风格基石组合使用。

### 2.1 资产类型 (Asset Type)

- **场景 (Scene):** `2D side-scrolling game background, wide-angle view, establishing shot.` (2D横版游戏背景，广角视图，定场镜头。)
- **可交互道具 (Interactive Prop):** `A single object, centered, game asset, prop design, on a neutral dark background.` (单个物体，居中，游戏资产，道具设计，置于中性深色背景上。)
- **物品栏图标 (Inventory Icon):** `Clean 2D icon, game asset, centered, on a transparent background, clear silhouette.` (简洁的2D图标，游戏资产，居中，透明背景，轮廓清晰。)

### 2.2 核心元素 (Core Elements)

- **线条与质感 (Line & Texture):** `strong ink outlines, cross-hatching shadows, etching style, visible paper texture, slightly smudged.` (粗墨水轮廓，交叉阴影，蚀刻风格，可见的纸张纹理，轻微污迹。)
- **色调 (Color Palette):** `dominant palette of dark browns, muted grays, and deep blacks, accented with [blood red / lake blue / dark gold].` (主色调为深棕、灰和黑色，以[血红/湖蓝/暗金]作为点缀。)
- **光影 (Lighting):** `a single harsh spotlight, casting long shadows, creating a sense of unease, eerie glow from an unseen source.` (一束刺眼的聚光灯，投下长长的影子，营造不安感，来自未知光源的诡异光芒。)
- **氛围 (Atmosphere):** `Victorian-era aesthetic, surreal and bizarre imagery, alchemical symbols, strange contraptions, grotesque yet fascinating.` (维多利亚时代美学，超现实和怪诞的图像，炼金术符号，奇怪的装置，怪诞而迷人。)

## 3. 实践应用示例 (Practical Application Examples)

### 示例1：第一章主场景 - 博物馆展厅

**目标:** 生成一个空旷的、可以放置各种谜题道具的博物馆展厅背景。

**组合提示词:**
`In the distinct art style of the Rusty Lake series, a detailed hand-drawn illustration with a printmaking texture. A 2D side-scrolling game background of a grand, decaying museum hall. The scene is characterized by a muted and desaturated color palette of dark browns and deep blacks, with dramatic chiaroscuro lighting from a single harsh spotlight on the center floor. Etching-style cross-hatching creates deep shadows in the corners. The hall is empty, featuring a blank back wall with space for display cases, and an empty floor area, creating a surreal and mysterious atmosphere. Visible paper texture, slightly smudged.`

**中文解析:**
`以锈湖系列独特的艺术风格，一幅细节丰富的手绘插画，带有版画质感。一个宏伟、腐朽的博物馆大厅的2D横版游戏背景。场景特点是柔和且低饱和度的深棕色和深黑色调，一束刺眼的聚光灯在地板中央形成戏剧性的明暗对比。蚀刻风格的交叉排线在角落里制造出深邃的阴影。大厅是空的，背景墙留有空白区域以备放置展柜，地板区域也是空的，营造出一种超现实和神秘的氛围。可见的纸张纹理，有轻微污迹。`

### 示例2：可交互道具 - 音乐盒 (关闭状态)

**目标:** 生成一个可以被玩家点击和交互的音乐盒。

**组合提示词:**
`In the distinct art style of the Rusty Lake series, a detailed hand-drawn illustration of a single object, a Victorian-era music box, centered on a neutral dark background. The music box is made of dark wood with tarnished brass fittings. The style is characterized by strong ink outlines and etching-style cross-hatching shadows. The color palette is muted and desaturated. This is a game asset prop design.`

**中文解析:**
`以锈湖系列独特的艺术风格，一幅细节丰富的手绘插画，描绘单个物体：一个维多利亚时代的音乐盒，位于中性深色背景中央。音乐盒由深色木材制成，带有失去光泽的黄铜配件。风格特点是粗墨水轮廓和蚀刻风格的交叉排线阴影。色调柔和且低饱和度。这是一个游戏资产道具设计。`

### 示例3：物品栏图标 - 真理之羽

**目标:** 生成一个可以放入玩家物品栏的“真理之羽”图标。

**组合提示词:**
`Clean 2D icon of a single pure white feather, in the distinct art style of the Rusty Lake series. The icon has a clear silhouette for inventory use, with strong ink outlines and minimal cross-hatching for texture. Centered on a transparent background. Game asset.`

**中文解析:**
`一个纯白色羽毛的简洁2D图标，具有锈湖系列独特的艺术风格。该图标为物品栏使用而设计，轮廓清晰，拥有粗墨水轮廓和最少的交叉排线纹理。位于透明背景中央。游戏资产。`
